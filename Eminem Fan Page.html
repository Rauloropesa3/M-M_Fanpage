From Battle Rapper to Legend




Bio

Born Marshall Bruce Mathers III in 1972 in Missouri, Eminem had a turbulent childhood. He dropped out of school in the ninth grade and worked odd jobs until finally making it as a rapper upon the release of The Slim Shady LP in early 1999.

Its been a while since Eminem hit the scene, but he still seems to suprise the industry with his wordplay and unmatched lyrics. You might not like his style,or his music, but when it comes to Rap you have to respect him. Many have tried to go against him and have failed, proving why he is the best MC of our time.
